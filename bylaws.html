LOC Bylaws

Updated: February 16th, 2021
  • Buy-In
    • $150
    • Due draft weekend before round 1
  • 3 Player Keeper League
    • Owners must keep 3 players
    • Once keepers are selected, only keepers and picks can be traded up to draft day
  • Roster Size
    • 21 players: 17 Starters, 4 Bench
      • C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, OF x3, Util x2, SP x4, RP x2, P x1, BN x4.
      • 2 closers is the max
      • 3 DL Spots
  • 6x6 Scoring
    • Hitting: Runs, HR, RBI, SB, AVG, OPS
    • Pitching: Wins, K, QS, SV, Whip, ERA
    • 32 Inning Minimum Per Matchup
  • Weekly Transaction Limit
    • 5 per week
    • 5 per playoff matchup
  • FAAB
    • $200 total for the regular season
    • $1 minimum bids
    • FAAB resets at start of playoffs
      • $35 in the playoffs and $23 for shit bowl
    • Tiebreaker is inverse order of standings
  • Regular Season
    • 18 week regular season
    • Play inside your division twice and outside once
    • The final two weeks are against the team that you played last in the previous season’s playoffs and a random draw against non division opponent
  • Playoffs
    • 8 Teams make the playoffs
      • Top 3 from each division
      • Final two are the two with the best record
    • Playoffs are 2 week matchups
    • Division winners receive the top 2 seeds, followed by record
    • Shit bowl is determined by record
    • Playoff Tiebreaker is determined by seed
  • Draft Order
    • Playoff Tiebreaker is determined by seed
    • Shit bowl first round losers play for picks 5 and 6
    • Playoff teams that get bounced in the first round play for picks 1, 2, 7 and 8
  • Payouts
    • Division winners and 3rd place: $150.
      • Teams do not receive this, it just pays for their buy-in next season
    • Second place: $450
      • League fee paid for next season leaves a net payout of $300
        • Exception if player was a division winner, thus already paid $150
    • First place: $900
      • League fee paid for next season leaves a net payout of $750
        • Exception if player was a division winner, thus already paid $150
  • Iceboxers
    • If you don’t make the playoffs and lose both rounds of the playoffs, it is mandatory that your team name is Iceboxers the following season with no abbreviations or modifications
    • This is in honor of one founding fathers and staunch republican Dan Snider
  • Danny Duffy Committee
    • Commish, Gaz and HB
    • If a player is injured while a trade is pending, the committee will determine if the receiving team of said play can DL the injured player
  • DL Preseason
    • As long as the player is drafted, he can be DL’d
  • Kerry Wood/Scumbag Jason Rule
    • No players can be added off the waiver wire and DL’d
  • Name Change Rule
    • Any name change after opening pitch is a $10 charge to next year’s buy-in
  • Prop Bets
    • $5 min/max
    • Can not have duplicate bets
    • Props will be added/subtracted to the following years buy-in
  • Pre-Season Trading
    • Any trade talk outside of indicated times will be considered tampering
    • If owner(s) are found tampering, owners will be subject to large fines, loss of draft pick and/or beatings
      • Punishment will be handed down from the commissioner’s office
  • Sloth Rule
    • When trades are made, they are processed by ESPN
    • The commish cannot push them though for any reason whatsoever
    • Trades will not be manually entered during the season unless it is a 3 team trade
    • For extended explanation, see June 28, 2015 thread about Gunnaz calling for a witch hunt on commish (I don't think this actually exists anymore)
  • Waivers
    • Waivers are Sunday and Wednesday at 10pm Central Time
  • Reggie Bush Rule
    • An owner can drop a player who has a serious injury
    • Drugs, suspension, etc. not allowed
    • Player is frozen on the waiver wire for the rest of the season
      • Said owner is required to keep him the following year
      • Player is not allowed to be traded until after the draft
  • Carlos Lee Rule
    • A player must play one game for owner to be eligible to be kept, unless he is traded for and deemed nearing return
      • Example would be 15 day DL, rehab assignment, etc.
      • This is up to commish ruling
      • Two Examples:
        • 2014 Commish trades for Tulo while on rehab assignment and has major setback
        • HB trading for Kershaw after reports of positive outcome after throwing
      • If a player is deemed “out for the season” he is not eligible to be kept
      • Exemption only applies to traded players
        • No players off waiver
